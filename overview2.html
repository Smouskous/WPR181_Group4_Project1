Fundamental Elements and Structure:

It is an essential component of the larger internet, a worldwide network of connected computer networks.

The World Wide Web is a complex ecosystem made up of many different parts, protocols, and technologies that all work together to offer the material and services we use every day.

The fundamental aspect of the internet is hyperlinks, which let users move between various web sites by clicking on items that may be clicked on inside a page.

URLs (Uniform Resource Locators): URLs are distinctive addresses that direct users to particular web resources, including web pages, photos, videos, and other kinds of material.
